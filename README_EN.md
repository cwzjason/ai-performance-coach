# AI Performance Review Coach

> Help new managers nail their first performance feedback conversation without a hitch вҖ” an interactive web-based simulation training tool.

## Overview

Performance reviews are one of the most critical вҖ” and risky вҖ” actions for any manager. Too soft and it's ineffective; too harsh and employees may quit; off-target and it becomes personal. **AI Performance Review Coach** lets you practice one-on-one performance conversations with AI-simulated "real employees" in a safe environment, then provides a multi-dimensional evaluation report and improvement suggestions afterward.

### Core Value

- **Zero-risk practice** вҖ” Rehearse in a virtual environment without fear of saying the wrong thing or damaging relationships
- **Realistic employee simulation** вҖ” AI dynamically generates emotional responses based on employee profiles (High-Potential / Steady / Needs Improvement)
- **Instant feedback** вҖ” Automatically generates a performance review quality score report with flagged pitfalls
- **Scenario-based training** вҖ” 5 common problem scenarios + custom backgrounds, covering 3Г—5=15 combinations

## рҹҡҖ How to Use

1. **Choose a scenario** вҖ” Select employee type, performance rating, and problem scenario in the left config panel
2. **Start the session** вҖ” Click "Start Practice" to begin the conversation
3. **Have the conversation** вҖ” Type your coaching phrases and press Enter; or use quick reply buttons
4. **Watch reactions** вҖ” Observe changes in employee mood (Tense вҶ’ Defensive вҶ’ Cautious вҶ’ Open вҶ’ Collaborative)
5. **End the session** вҖ” Click "End & Score" or say "end the review" in conversation
6. **Read the report** вҖ” Review your evaluation to understand strengths and areas for improvement
7. **Practice again** вҖ” Click "New Session" to try a different scenario

## рҹ’Ў Coaching Tips at a Glance

> Review = Connect вҶ’ Present facts вҶ’ Reach consensus

1. **Start with empathy**: "I've noticed you've been working late a lot lately вҖ” thank you. How have things been feeling?"
2. **Ask open-ended questions**: "What's your take?" "What do you think might be causing this?"
3. **Give feedback with specifics**: "For example, the XX requirement last week..."
4. **Co-create the plan**: "Let's set goals for the next two weeks вҖ” where do you think we could start?"
5. **Link to personal growth**: "Getting this right will really help your career path"
